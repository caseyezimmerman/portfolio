About Me
Hi, I'm Casey

I am a full-stack developer living in Atlanta, Georgia. I am from Marietta, GA and attended school at the University of Mississippi where I got my Bachelors of Science in Exercise Science. After graduating, I moved back to Atlanta and worked at a small accounting firm as an office manager. After working there for a year I knew that I needed to make a career change. I love working in a fast paced environment and being able to constantly learn new things every day, so I thought software development would be the perfect challenge for me.

Per my brother's recomendation, I started taking some classes on Coursera and Udemy and I instantly fell in love with it. I decided that programming was something that I needed to persue so I decided to face the scary, yet exciting challenge of a coding bootcamp. I have recently graduated from DigitCrafts' 4 month immersive coding bootcamp and I wouldn't change it for the world. Now I am able to build awesome things!

City Runner

A web app for runners that generates running loops around them based on the user's current location and desired distance in miles. The app renders 4 maps for the user to choose from and then their data (time and distance) is displayed on a results page when they finish their run.

This is an ecommerce site made with react and redux. It is a spin-off of a petsmart type site for purchasing food, toys, treats and more online. Functionality includes a secure register and login page, a purcahse button that only appears when a customer is logged in, and a shopping cart page that is connected with stripe so the customer can checkout.
