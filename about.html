Hi, I'm Casey

I am a full-stack developer living in Atlanta, Georgia. I am from Marietta, GA and went to school at the University of Mississippi where I got my Bachelors of Science in Exercise Science. After graduating I moved back to Atlanta and worked at a small accounting firm as an office manager. After working there for a year I knew that I needed to make a career change. I love working in a fast paced environment and constantly learning new things, so I thought software development would be the perfect challenge for me.

I started taking some classes on Coursera and Udemy and I instantly loved it. I decided that programming was something that I needed to persue so I decided to attend a coding bootcamp. I will be graduating from DigitCrafts' 4 month immersive coding bootcamp in January 2018.
